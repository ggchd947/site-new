USA is a Meritocracy

People don’t want to admit it but the USA is a meritocracy.

If you have:

  • energy / work ethic
  • a sense of urgency
  • communication skills
  • ability to lead / delegate

You will get rich.

Any background, any cit or town, any culture, any race.
